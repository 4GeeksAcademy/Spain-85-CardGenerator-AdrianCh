The house always has the edge in blackjack, and here’s why: Because you act first. If you bust, you lose—even if the dealer busts too. Dealers also play by strict rules, while players choices can lead to mistakes, which tilt the odds in the houses favor.

Congratulations! Beating the odds and winning against the house is no small feat. Remember, in blackjack, smart choices and strategy can help you win—but luck is always a factor too. Enjoy the victory and keep playing smart to tip the odds in your favor!
Tie!
You matched the dealers hand, so your bet is safe. Keep in mind, though, that casinos gain an edge because if you bust, you lose—even if the dealer does too. Ties keep you in the game, but the house still has the advantage. Play smart to tip the odds!
